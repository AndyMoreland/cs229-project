Upload Your Old IHUM Essays

About This

This is part of a CS229 project by Charlie Guo and Andy Moreland.

The goal of our project is to develop an algorithm that can automatically grade IHUM essays.

In order to accomplish this, we need the essays and grades of past IHUM students.

How to Help

Simply enter your email below and click the green button.

On the next screen, upload your essays and enter the grades that you received alongside the corresponding essay.

We will not store any personally identifying information from the documents, but feel free to remove your name before submitting.

Step 1.

Click "Upload New Essay"

Step 2.

Select essay from your computer, or from other services you may use

Step 3.

Choose grade, and repeat. If you are unsure, select your best guess.
